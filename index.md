## 🎯 Featured Projects

### 🇦🇹 Mein Wien - Vienna Travel Guide
**Your Complete Guide to Vienna, Austria**

A comprehensive, tourist-friendly travel guide to Vienna with curated Wikipedia sources, historical insights, and practical travel information.

- 📍 **Location**: Northeastern Austria on the Danube River
- 🏛️ **History**: From Roman Vindobona through the Habsburg Empire to modern times
- 🎫 **Must-See**: Schönbrunn Palace, St. Stephen's Cathedral, Hofburg
- 🎵 **Culture**: 100+ museums, UNESCO coffee culture, classical music legacy
- 🍷 **Gastronomy**: Schnitzel, Sachertorte, authentic Heuriger wine taverns

**Status**: 🏋🏼‍♂️ Work in Progress | Research began June 20, 2026 | Trip: August 15-17, 2026
