# Mein Wien 🇦🇹
## Your Complete Guide to Vienna, Austria

Welcome to **Mein Wien** - a comprehensive travel guide to one of Europe's most beautiful and culturally rich cities. Whether you're planning your first visit or returning to explore more, this guide offers insights into Vienna's history, attractions, and hidden gems.

---

## 📍 About Vienna

Vienna (Wien) is the capital and largest city of Austria, located in the northeastern part of the country. It's a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for:

- **Historical Significance**: From Roman times (Vindobona) through the Habsburg Empire
- **Cultural Hub**: 100+ world-class museums and galleries
- **Architectural Wonder**: Stunning palaces, cathedrals, and Art Nouveau buildings
- **Musical Legacy**: Home to Mozart, Beethoven, and Strauss

## ✨ Why Visit Vienna?

- 🏛️ **Imperial History**: Walk through centuries of Habsburg grandeur
- 🎵 **Music & Culture**: Experience classical music in historic concert halls
- ☕ **Coffee Culture**: UNESCO-recognized Viennese coffeehouse tradition
- 🍷 **Wine Regions**: Nearby wine-producing areas (Heurigen)
- 🎡 **Modern Attractions**: Prater Ferris wheel, modern art galleries
- 🍽️ **Culinary Excellence**: Schnitzel, Sachertorte, and local delicacies
