# 🇦🇹 Mein Wien
## Your Complete Travel Guide to Vienna, Austria

[🇦🇹 Deutsch](./wien-de.html) | [🇫🇷 Français](./wien-fr.html) | [🇮🇹 Italiano](./wien-it.html) | [🇬🇧 English](./wien.html)

Welcome to **Mein Wien** - a comprehensive travel guide to one of Europe's most beautiful, historically rich, and culturally vibrant cities. Vienna (Wien) has captivated visitors for centuries with its imperial palaces, world-class museums, and legendary coffeehouse culture.

---

## 📜 Yesterday is History

Vienna's history spans from Roman times as *Vindobona* through its evolution as one of Europe's most powerful capital cities. The city became the center of the Austro-Hungarian Empire, one of history's great powers that dominated vast territories from the 16th to 20th centuries.

**Key Historical Highlights:**
- **Roman Era**: Founded as Vindobona (1st century AD)
- **Ottoman Siege (1683)**: A turning point in European history
- **Golden Age (18th-19th centuries)**: Rise to prominence under the Habsburg Dynasty
- **Vienna Congress (1815)**: Reshaped Europe after Napoleon
- **Austro-Hungarian Era**: Center of art, music, and intellectual thought
- **Post-WWI**: Transition to modern Austria

Vienna's architecture reflects centuries of imperial heritage—from medieval churches and Baroque palaces to stunning Art Nouveau (Secession-style) buildings.

---

## 🌟 Tomorrow is a Mystery

Located on the Danube River, Vienna enchants visitors with:

### 🏛️ **Iconic Attractions**
- **Schönbrunn Palace**: Former imperial summer residence with stunning gardens
- **St. Stephen's Cathedral (Stephansdom)**: Gothic masterpiece standing since 1365
- **Hofburg Palace**: Historic winter residence of Austrian emperors
- **Ringstrasse**: Elegant boulevard lined with cultural institutions

### 🎵 **Cultural Treasures**
- Vienna State Opera House - legendary classical music venue
- Cradle of classical music (Mozart, Beethoven, Strauss)
- 100+ world-class museums (more per capita than any European city)
- Vibrant arts and theater scene

### ☕ **Authentic Experiences**
- UNESCO-recognized Viennese coffeehouse culture
- Traditional Heuriger wine taverns
- Historic markets and charming cobblestone streets
- Local Viennese cuisine (Schnitzel, Sachertorte, Strudel)

---

## 🎁 Today is a Gift - Fun Facts

✨ **Vienna's Superlatives:**

- **Most Livable City**: Voted by The Economist Intelligence Unit multiple times
- **Museum Capital**: Over 100 museums—more per capita than any other European city
- **Musical Heritage**: The Vienna Philharmonic's New Year's Concert broadcasts to 90+ countries
- **UNESCO Treasures**: Viennese coffeehouse culture is protected as Intangible Cultural Heritage
- **Iconic Architecture**: The colorful Hundertwasserhaus by artist Friedensreich Hundertwasser
- **Historic Landmarks**: St. Stephen's Cathedral has survived wars, fires, and revolutions since 1365
- **Ferris Wheel**: The famous Prater Wheel opened in 1766 and features in "The Third Man" film
- **Wine Production**: One of few European capitals producing its own wine
- **Public Transport**: Among Europe's best and most efficient transit systems
- **Famous Residents**: Sigmund Freud, Gustav Klimt, and countless musical geniuses
